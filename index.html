About Us:
Inspiration for this idea came when we saw that people with great ideas are sometimes reluctant to start a business simply because they aren't sure if the market would think that their idea is a good one; moreover, before becoming an adult, some of the only people someone could get advice from would be their parents and friends, both of whom give skewed advice. Our target audience is any entrepreneur who wishes to test the market but has no easy means of doing so. The features we are most proud of are our submission and up vote/down vote systems.
